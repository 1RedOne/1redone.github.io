Work around

There is actually an issue in that your account may have been used on many PCs over the years...or maybe like me you upgraded a major component and that trigegred you to log back in, whcih then pushed you over the limit.  The issue might be, if you have a very old Microsoft account like me (been using it and Xbox logins since the Windows 8 era) that your Windows Store logins may not be properly merged in to your Xbox / windows store profile.  If this happens, when you click the link to view your managed devices whcih is shwon in Minecraft, you'll see an empty list!
